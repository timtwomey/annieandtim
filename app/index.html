Venue

The wedding will take place in Chateau de Cautine, near the town of Saint-Privat, Coreze, France.Situated on the edge of the Dordogne region of France, the chateau will play host to our wedding and reception.

Travel

There are a number of flight options depending on where you're coming from. From Dublin, Aer Lingus flies to Bordeaux and Toulouse. Ryanair flies to Rodez, which is closer. From London there are additional options with Ryanair, flying to Brive la Gaillarde, Limougues and Bergerac. Note that not all flights run on daily schedules. Also, as we're planning on a mid-afternoon celebration, there is the option of getting flights on the morning of the 13th.

If you'd rather drive over, Irish Ferries and Brittany Ferries operate routes from Ireland to either Roscoff or Cherbourg. From the UK there are more options again, including the Eurotunnel.

Once on French soil, the most preferable mode of transport would be to hire a car. From the main airports listed, expect the drive to be between an hour or three depending on distance.

  • Argus Car Hire

Taking the train or bus is also an option from the main cities. By train, to Brive la Gaillarde or Aurillac from London via Paris the journey takes 8 hours, then bus to the village. There are 2 buses a day from outside Brive station to St Privat: 14.30 and 18.20pm. There are no buses on Sunday or public holidays. Bus fare: €2 and we can pick you up from the village.

  • Bus Timetable

If you are in need of transportation in France, please give us your flight details and we can assess if there is demand for organising a private bus. Car rental is recommended however, as the area is quiet remote and there are many sights you may want to visit which can only be reached by car.
